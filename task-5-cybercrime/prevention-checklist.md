# Stay Safe Online Checklist

1. Never share OTP, UPI PIN, or CVV
2. Verify QR codes before scanning
3. Enable two-factor authentication on important accounts like email, banking, and social media.
4. Avoid using the same password across multiple platforms.
5. Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities especially for banking, college portals, or logins.
6. Keep apps and devices updated
7. Avoid sharing phone numbers, addresses, or daily routines publicly.
8. Use trusted apps only like Google Pay or PhonePe and avoid unofficial APK downloads.

**Report Cyber Crime in India**
1. Report on the National Cyber Crime Portal at https://cybercrime.gov.in
2. Call Cyber Helpline: 1930 (the faster you act, the higher the chance of recovering money)
3. Inform your bank within minutes to freeze transactions
